Ephaphatha Construction (Pty) Ltd

About Us
Ephaphatha Construction Pty Ltd is a versatile and innovative company dedicated to delivering high-quality services across multiple sectors. Based in Bloemfontein, we serve businesses, households, and public institutions with a commitment to excellence and reliability. Our diverse service portfolio enables us to meet a broad range of client needs, ensuring satisfaction, professionalism, and long-term relationships with all our clients.

Vision & Mission
Vision: Our vision is to establish Ephaphatha Construction Pty Ltd as a premier provider in general construction, maintenance, and supply services in South Africa, recognized for our integrity, quality, and customer-centric approach.

Mission: To provide efficient, cost-effective, and high-quality services that enhance the living and working environments of our clients. We aim to become a trusted partner for individuals and businesses in Bloemfontein and beyond, contributing positively to our community through our work.

Services
General Building: Our team offers reliable building services for both new constructions and renovations, handling every aspect of the building process with attention to detail.

Plumbing Services: From routine maintenance to full installations, our plumbing team provides quick, efficient, and affordable services.

Fencing Solutions: We offer durable and secure fencing installations tailored to each client's needs, enhancing both security and aesthetic appeal.

Paving: Specializing in both residential and commercial paving, we deliver attractive and long-lasting paved areas suited to a variety of environments.

Electrical Maintenance and Installations: Skilled in electrical maintenance, we offer wiring, repairs, and installation services to enhance safety and functionality.

Painting and Waterproofing: We offer painting and waterproofing services to protect and beautify properties, using high-quality materials and professional techniques.

Stationery Supplies: We provide a range of office and educational stationery supplies for schools, businesses, and individuals, ensuring consistent quality and value.

Furniture Supply: We supply a wide range of furniture for homes, offices, and schools, focusing on style, comfort, and durability.

Cleaning Services: Our cleaning team provides comprehensive cleaning services for homes, offices, and commercial properties, delivering a spotless and healthy environment.

Landscaping and Garden Services: Our landscaping team transforms outdoor spaces with professional garden design, maintenance, and plant supply.

Waste Removal and Site Clearing: We provide efficient waste management solutions and site clearing services for residential, commercial, and construction sites.

General Building Maintenance: We handle general building maintenance, ensuring properties remain functional, safe, and attractive for owners and users.

Meat Supply: We supply high-quality meat products, catering to the needs of restaurants, canteens, and individual clients.
